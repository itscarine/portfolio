About me

I live in Melbourne, Australia. I was born and raised in the Netherlands and have spent the past years living in Copenhagen, Sydney, and London.

My story

    Early 2020 I took charge of the redesign of a payment integration in response to COVID-19. It was then, after a detour through anthropology and photography, that the puzzle pieces finally fell into place and I realised I wanted to focus my career on product design. Since then, I've been able to express my passion to create lasting experiences through various projects.
    My spare time is filled with taking photos, running, yoga, hosting dinner parties, designing coffee table books, and (some may say) too much time on Pinterest.

Relevant Skills

    I currently work as a product designer. Three things that you'll notice when working with me are a passion for people, my excellent eye for detail, and my ability to go above and beyond.
